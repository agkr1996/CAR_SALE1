The Santa Fe is about helping your drive become a safer one. An array of available driver assistance technologies actively scans your surroundings to help keep you safe on your journeys. They’ve been developed to help alert you to, and even avoid, unexpected dangers on the road and include the world’s first available Safe Exit Assist1 technology. At the core of the Santa Fe is the SUPERSTRUCTURE™, a state-of-the-art vehicle frame built with our Advanced High-Strength Steel for superior rigidity and strength. In the event that a collision cannot be avoided, this ultra-rigid frame helps protect occupants by absorbing harmful energy and redirecting it away from the passenger compartment. Surround yourself with safety in the 2020 Santa Fe.
